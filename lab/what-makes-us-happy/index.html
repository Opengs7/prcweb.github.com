What makes us happy?

Is there a connection between our well-being and economic/social factors such as GDP?

This scatterplot plots the well-being of 22 European countries against 10 different categories. Start by clicking the categories above and see what trends you can identify.

Well-being data is from the New Economics Foundation's National Accounts of Well-being and the category data is from Gapminder.
